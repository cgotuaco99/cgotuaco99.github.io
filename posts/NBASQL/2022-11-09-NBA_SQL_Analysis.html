Now we will attempt to determine when players reach their primes using PER as our measure.

PER_test = ps.sqldf("""SELECT Player_name, Pos, MAX(PER) AS max_per, Year, Age, year_start, (Year-year_start) AS years_exp
            FROM yearinc
            WHERE G >= 58
            GROUP BY Player
            ORDER BY MAX(PER) DESC
            LIMIT 300;
            """)
